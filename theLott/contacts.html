What is a standard Oz Lotto ticket?
A standard ticket in Oz Lotto is made up of 7 main numbers. Each standard game gives you one chance of winning a prize. Game numbers can be chosen manually or automatically using the QuickPick function. Your 7 main numbers will be between 1 and 47 inclusive.
